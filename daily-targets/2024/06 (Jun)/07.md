      - `0 0 0 60px #2F9947`: A shadow with a spread radius of `60px` and the color `#2F9947`, a lighter green.
      - `0 0 0 500px #49C85B`: A shadow with a spread radius of `500px` and the color `#49C85B`, an even lighter green.
